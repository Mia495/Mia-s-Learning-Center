Jellyfish

  • Let's Learn

Jellyfish are sea animals with a soft, jellylike body and no bones. They
have tentacles that they use to sting their prey. Sometimes they sting
swimming people, too. Jellyfish are related to corals and sea anemones.
Some jellyfish can glow in the dark. They are the oldest multi-organ animal.
Theyc can be found all over the world. Some jellyfish are immortal. There is
even a giant jellyfish called the hair jelly. Over 150 million people are stung
by jellyfish each year.
