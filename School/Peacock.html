Peacock

  • Let's Learn

The peacock is the most exquisite bird in the world because
of its vibrant feathers and colour. The peacock spreads out
its colorful feathers like a fan to attract female peacocks,
also known as peahens. Peahens are usually a brown-ish color
with white bellies. Peacocks do not lay eggs, but peahens do.
A peahen's eggs are usually white with some dark spots.
