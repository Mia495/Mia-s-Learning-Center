Ostrich

  • Let's Learn

Ostriches are flightless birds with a long, bare neck, long, sturdy
legs and a bulky body covered with feathers. Males and females
have different coloured feathers. Males have black plumage with
a white tail, and females are mostly brown. They have two strong
legs that were made for speed and can run about 31 miles per hour
for a long time. If they're in a hurry, they can run short distances
up to 43 miles per hour. They will run from a predator, but they
can also fight with their powerful legs.
