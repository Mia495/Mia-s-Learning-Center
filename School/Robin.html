Robin

  • Let's Learn

Robins are very popular birds both here in North America,
as well as in Europe. Female robins build nests from the
inside out, using twigs and dead grass to form a cup shape.
They then reinforce the nest with mud from worm castings.
Only male robins sing a song to mark their territory, and it
sounds like "cheer-up, cheerily, cheer-up, cheerily".
