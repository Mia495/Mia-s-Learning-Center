Lion

  • Let's Learn

The lion is the largest member of the cat family. Female lions
are the main hunters. While they're out looking for food, the
males guard the pride's territory and their young. A lion's roar
can be heard up to eight kilometres away. The lion has a long
body, short legs, sharp claws, and a large head. Adult males
are about 9 to 10 feet long, including the tail. They stand about
3 feet tall at the shoulder and can weigh 370 to 500 pounds.
