Newt

  • Let's Learn

Newts look a lot like frogs, but also like fish. Newts have both
legs and tails on their bodies. A newt is a small, brightly
colored amphibian that spends part of its time on land and
the rest in the water. A newt looks like a cross between a
frog and a lizard. Newts are a type of salamander that primarily
lives on land but also has gills that enable it to occasionally
live an aquatic life. The newt has a long, slender body with a
laterally flattened tail. The largest European type grows to about
7 inches in length. The largest American type, the California
newt, reaches about 6 inches.
