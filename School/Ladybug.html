Ladybug

  • Let's Learn

Ladybugs are small and usually quite round in shape. The color on the wing
covers can be yellow, orange, or red and often has small black dots on it.
Some species are solid black. Ladybugs also have black legs, head, and
antennae. Their spots act as a warning for predators. When prey is in short
supply, female ladybugs may eat their own eggs which they've laid. Ladybugs
can lay up to 1,000 eggs in one season. They have tiny wings underneath their
red shell, which are used to fly.
