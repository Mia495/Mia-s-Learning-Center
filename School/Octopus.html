Octopus

  • Let's Learn

Octopuses are sea animals famous for their rounded bodies, bulging eyes, and
eight long arms. Octopus has two eyes and a beak, and they have very good
eyesight. Octopuses are also known for their intelligence, in fact they are
considered to be one of the most intelligent invertebrates in the world as
they demonstrate many behaviours. Octopuses will sometimes hunt with
other animals, usually fish. Octopuses are so smart that if they are kept
in an aquarium they will sometimes learn how to escape, and they will
sometimes use this knowledge to free other octopuses.
