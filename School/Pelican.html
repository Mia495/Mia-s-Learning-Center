Pelican

  • Let's Learn

Pelicans are among the largest flying birds. They are famous
for their huge throat pouches. A pelican uses its pouch and
its very long bill to scoop up fish. The color of pelicans depends
on the species, but they all have short legs, webbed feet, and
large wings. Pelicans live around lakes, rivers, and seacoasts in
many parts of the world.
