Narwhal

  • Let's Learn

The narwhal is a medium-sized toothed whale that has a large tusk, protruding
from a canine tooth. These warm-blooded, air-breathing mammals, are often
nicknamed the 'unicorn of the sea' because of the tusk. Some narwhals can
even have two tusks, while others don't have one at all. A narwhal's tusk
can be up to 9 feet long and weigh more than 22 pounds. Narwhal can dive
really deep into the ocean. They are one of the deepest diving sea mammals.
They can dive up to 5,000 feet deep. Narwhals can live to be 50 years old.
