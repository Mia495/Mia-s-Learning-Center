Moth

  • Let's Learn

The moth is easily recognised by the round-tipped forewing, with
large, rounded, pale-outlined oval and kidney mark and uniformly
grey hindwing. Their markings are a shade of brown or grey. This moth
is the only one of its kind in its genus here in North America. Green
caterpillars eat from a variety of plants. Ash, birch, crabapple, maple,
and oak leaves are abundant food sources. Grape leaves also make a
good host plant.
