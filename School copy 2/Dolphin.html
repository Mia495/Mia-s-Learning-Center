Dolphin

  • Let's Learn

Dolphins are usually colored in some mixture of black, white, and gray.
They have two flippers, or fins, on their sides, as well as a triangular
fin on the back. A dolphin must come up to the water's surface to get
air. It breathes through a single nostril, called a blowhole, on the top
of its head. Dolphins have a long, slender snout with about 100 teeth,
and a streamlined body. They can be found all over the world and in
different environments.
