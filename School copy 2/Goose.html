  • Let's Learn

Geese eat seeds, nuts, grass, plants and berries. They are known
to be the largest waterfowls. Geese have humped bills that become
narrower toward the tip. Their bills are good for grasping grasses,
which is their main food. Though they are waterfowls, they spent
most of their time on land. Geese always fly in the shape of a 'V',
as it allows at least 71 % greater flying range.
