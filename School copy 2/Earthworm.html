Earthworm

  • Let's Learn

Earthworms are smooth-skinned worms, with a body made of many small segments.
If you look at the body you can see lines that mark the segments. Each segment
has stiff little hairs that help the worm move. But, sometimes the hairs are
hard to see. They have no skeleton, but they do have lots of muscles. Earthworms
eat almost anything that is alive, as long as it is dead when they eat it. They
use their skin to breathe as they do not have lungs.
