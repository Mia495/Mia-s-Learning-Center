Elephant

  • Let's Learn

Elephants are known for their large ears, tusks made of ivory, and their trunks.
Elephants are the world's largest land animals. They can reach 3m tall and
weigh between 4,000kg (kilograms). Elephants can use their trunks to pick up
heavy objects, trumpet warnings, greet other elephants, or suck up water for
drinking or bathing, among other uses.
