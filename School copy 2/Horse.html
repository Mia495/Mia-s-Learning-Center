Horse

  • Let's Learn

Horses are large and very strong. A typical domesticated horse stands 5 feet
tall at the shoulder. It weighs about 1,150 pounds. A horse's legs are
strong even though they look very slender.Horses have oval-shaped hooves,
long tails, short hair, long slender legs, muscular and deep torso build,
long thick necks, and large elongated heads. The mane is a region of coarse
hairs, which extends along the dorsal side of the neck in both domestic
and wild species.
