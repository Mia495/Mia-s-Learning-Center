Hummingbird

  • Let's Learn

In Native American cultures, hummingbirds are revered for their
keen sense of sight and ability to fly at great heights. The
red-tailed hummingbird's average weight is three pounds. Females
are about 25-percent heavier than males. hummingbirds are solitary
birds who spend much of their time hunting for food or
resting. As birds of prey, they eat only meat.
