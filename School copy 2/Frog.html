Frog

  • Let's Learn

A frog has smooth, moist skin and big, bulging eyes. Its hind legs are more than
twice as long as its front ones. Most frogs have webbed back feet to help them
leap and swim. Tree frogs have sticky disks on the tips of their fingers and
toes.Frogs are amphibians, which means they're able to live on land and in
water. They have striking characteristics, like protruding eyes, webbed feet
and their skin is smooth and moist. Most of them live in water, but some species
of frog live in trees or burrow down into the ground.
