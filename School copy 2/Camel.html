Camel

  • Let's Learn

A camel is a large animal with a long neck and one or two humps on its back, and they're
designed to survive in extreme desert conditions. These animals come in many shades
of brown, from cream-colored to dark brown, and a camel can be over 7 feet tall at
its hump.They are the only land mammals capable of drinking salt water without any
ill effects. They can drink up to fifty-seven litres of water in one go. It's a myth
that camels store water in their humps. They have sealable nostrils, to stop the sand
getting up their nose.
