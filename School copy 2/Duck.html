Duck

  • Let's Learn

A duck is a small type of swimming bird with a short neck and legs. They have webbed
feet to help them swim and broad and flat bills, which they use to feed. They can
live in freshwater or seawater and their feathers are waterproof. The duck is one
of the most beautiful birds found on our earth. It is mainly located near river
banks, streams, water pools, and lakes. The duck has a long beak. It is found in
different colors like golden, white, or brown.
