Giraffe

  • Let's Learn

Giraffes have a short body, but their legs and neck are very long. The coat is
light brown with reddish brown spots. Many giraffes have two short horns
between the ears. Giraffes have large nostrils and a good sense of smell.
The giraffe is the tallest of all mammals. It reaches an overall height of
18 feet or more. The legs and neck are extremely long. Giraffes are social
animals, living in loose, open, unstable herds varying from 10 to 20 indivi-
duals, although herds of up to 70 have been observed. Giraffes feed and drink
during the morning and evening.
