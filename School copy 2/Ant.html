Ant

  • Let's Learn

Ants are social insects and like to live in big colonies. They
live in nests all over the world. Ants can live underground,
in wood, plants, trees and soil. They cleverly use soil and
plants to help make their nests. Ants have six legs and three
main body parts: head, thorax, and abdomen. Some also have
wings. They live together in groups called colonies, and every
ant has a job. Ants go through metamorphosis, or changes, as
they grow from eggs to larvae to pupae to adults.
