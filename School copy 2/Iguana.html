Iguana

  • Let's Learn

Iguanas are reptiles that can be lots of different sizes. They come in
different colors like green, brown, or gray in color. Iguanas live
in warm places like deserts, islands, and rain forests, and they
lay their eggs in underground burrows. Iguanas are herbivores that
eat algae, flower buds, and leaves. Iguanas are sociable reptiles
that live in groups. They live in rainforest areas, like in Mexico,
South America and the Caribbean. Iguanas can range in colour, but
they are typically bright green, yellow or grey. Some of them have
hints of orange and yellow, too.
