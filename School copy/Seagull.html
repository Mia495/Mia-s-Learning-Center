Seagull

  • Let's Learn

Seagulls can land on the water to feed or rest. They are
expert food thieves. Seagulls are opportunistic feeders,
meaning they will try almost any food they encounter.
Seagulls can migrate, meaning that they spend different
seasons in different places. They have incredible memories
too, they can identify human faces and behaviours especially
those who have previously fed them.
