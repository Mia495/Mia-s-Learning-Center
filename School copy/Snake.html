Snake

  • Let's Learn

A snake is a reptile with a long, thin body but no legs.
Some lizards are legless and look like snakes, however,
most lizards have eyelids, and snakes do not. All snakes
eat live prey, which is grabbed and swallowed whole
without chewing it into pieces. Their ears are inside them
and not outside, like ours. Snakes cannot see the way other
animals do. The vision for most snake species is blurry.
This is why snakes tend to rely on their other senses to
hunt and move about.
