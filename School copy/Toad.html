toads have some fish-like features that may not be found in
adult amphibians such as a lateral line, gills and swimming tails.
Most toads are vegetarians, although those of a few species
are carnivorous or even cannibalistic. toad metamorphosis
follows a pattern of gradual development of forelimbs and hind
limbs, resorption of the tail, shortening of the intestine, disappearance
of the gills, and development of lungs.
